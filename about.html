About

College Drive LLC

College Drive Consulting

Valeria Skarbek, founder of College Drive LLC, has over 20 years experience working with students and adults in K-12 and higher education settings including in a college and graduate admissions office. As a College and Career Prep teacher, she has worked with hundreds of students on their college lists, helped them research their potential majors and careers, and guided students as they write their resumes and college essays.

Qualifications

Val is completing her certificate as an Independent Educational Consultant through University of California, Irvine and will finish in December of 2023. She earned her B.S.B.A. in Marketing and Advertising and her Master of Arts in Teaching (M.A.T.). She earned her Master of Library Science (M.L.S.) from Southern Connecticut State University in New Haven, CT. She also earned a graduate certificate in Online Teaching and Learning from Boise State University.

About College Drive LLC

As a life-long learner, Val firmly believes in helping students discover how their passions connect to their future careers and college majors. She recognizes the uniqueness of each student and respects their individuality. She loves traveling with her family in their RV and touring colleges whenever she can.
Val is a member of the Independent Educational Consultants Association (IECA), College Consultants of Colorado (CCC), and the Rocky Mountain Association for College Admission Counseling (RMACAC).
