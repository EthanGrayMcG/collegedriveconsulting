Services

Guiding today's generation on the road to college

SERVICES

Initial Consultation

During the free first appointment, Val meets with the student and the parent(s) to discuss goals, timelines, schedules, questions, etc. There is no charge for this 30 minute strategy session.


Freshman/Sophmore

Val meets with the student every 3-4 months with freshmen or on a bi-monthly basis with sophomores to discuss the student’s extracurricular activities, interests, passions, course selection options, grades, and explore summer opportunities in their area of interest as they build a list of possible majors, careers, and colleges.

New Client Special: $750 + Junior Package $4495 (Regularly $1000 + $4995)
Learn more

Juniors

Val meets with the student every month during Junior year and more often during Senior year to craft a list of possible careers, majors, and colleges. Val will also help the student prepare for the SATs and investigate whether the student should also take the ACTs. Val will help the student prepare an academic resume and explore extra curricular and summer opportunities based on the student’s interests.

New Client Special: $4495 (Regularly $4995)
Learn more

Senior Sprint

Val meets with the student as needed throughout senior year to help finalize their college list, work on personal essays and prompts, apply to schools and scholarships, compare financial aid packages, and assist with making the final decision on where to attend college.

About College Drive LLC

Valeria Skarbek, founder of College Drive LLC, has over 20 years experience working with students and adults in K-12 and high education settings including in a college and graduate admissions office. As a College and Career Prep teacher, she has worked with hundreds of students on their college lists, helped them research their potential majors and careers, and guided students as they write their resumes and college essays.
