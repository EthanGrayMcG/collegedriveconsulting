About College Drive LLC

Valeria Skarbek, founder of College Drive LLC, has over 20 years experience working with students and adults in K-12 and higher education settings including in a college and graduate admissions office. As a College and Career Prep teacher, she has worked with hundreds of students on their college lists, helped them research their potential majors and careers, and guided students in writing their resumes and college essays.
